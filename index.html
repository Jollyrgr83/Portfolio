event scoring database

This is the beginning of a larger project that I hope to have completed later this year. A public power utility in the southeast organizes a lineworkers rodeo event every year that consists of two tiers of competitors, each completing five events that receive an associated score and time. Calculating and reporting the overall scores has historically been a challenge due to the amount and type of data and the need for tie-breaking. At completion, this project will record, analyze, and display data as well as generate the necessary reports for this event.
